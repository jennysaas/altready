Frequently Asked Questions

Why does alt text matter for my business?

Missing alt text is the #1 accessibility failure cited in ADA lawsuits. With the EU Accessibility Act now in effect, commercial websites must be accessible. Alt text also improves SEO — images with proper alt text get up to 70% more clicks in image search.

How is AltReady different from AI alt text generators?

AI tools like AltText.ai charge per image (2-4¢ each) and send your images to external servers. AltReady is a management tool — it helps you find, audit, and bulk-edit alt text without per-image costs and without sending data off your server. Different tools for different needs.

Does it work with WooCommerce?

Yes. AltReady scans all WordPress content including WooCommerce product images, gallery images, and variation images. Perfect for stores with large product catalogs.

Does it work with page builders?

Yes. AltReady scans your WordPress media library directly, so it works regardless of your page builder — Elementor, Divi, Gutenberg, WPBakery, Classic Editor, and more.

Will it slow down my site?

No. AltReady runs only in your WordPress admin when you use it. It adds zero overhead to your frontend — your visitors won't notice any difference.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. AltReady runs 100% locally on your WordPress server. No data is sent anywhere — no cloud processing, no external APIs, no tracking. Built in Europe, GDPR compliant by design.

What happens when my license expires?

The plugin continues to work. You just won't receive updates or support until you renew. Your data and settings are never affected.

Can I use it on multiple sites?

Each license covers one WordPress installation. Need multiple sites? Contact us for multi-site pricing.
