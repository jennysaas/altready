Frequently Asked Questions

What is alt text and why does it matter?

Alt text (alternative text) describes images for screen readers used by visually impaired visitors. It's required for WCAG/ADA compliance and helps with SEO ‚Äî Google uses alt text to understand your images.

Yes! AltReady scans your WordPress media library directly, so it works regardless of which page builder or theme you use ‚Äî Elementor, Divi, Gutenberg, Classic Editor, and more.
